Movie Webpage

Don't Look Up

Director: Adam McKay

Synopsis: Two low-level astronomers must go on a giant media tour to warn mankind of an approaching comet that will destroy planet Earth.

Personal Review: I personally believe "Don't Look Up" was a really good movie. It was sad, funny, and just a bit weird. I love how it shows how friendship begins and how important friendship is.

Actor List

  • Leonardo DiCaprio - Dr. Randall Mindy
  • Jennifer Lawrence - Kate Dibiasky
  • Meryl Streep - President Orlean

Star Wars: The Clone Wars

Director: George Lucas

Synopsis: Chronicling the events that took place between Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones (2002) and Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith (2005), this show follows the wartime days of Anakin Skywalker (Matt Lanter), Obi-wan Kenobi (James Arnold Taylor), and Yoda (Tom Kane). This show also brings new characters to the forefront of Star Wars canon, including Anakin's apprentice Ahsoka Tano (Ashley Eckstein), and Captain Rex (Dee Bradley Baker).

Personal Review: Star Wars is my life and I can't wait for the next one to come out.

Actor List

  • Tom Kane - Narrator
  • Dee Bradley Baker - Clone Troopers
  • Matt Lanter - Anakin Skywalker
